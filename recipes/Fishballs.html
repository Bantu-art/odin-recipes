Fishballs

Description

Fishballs are a popular Asian street food made by blending fish paste with seasonings, forming into balls, and typically deep-frying until golden brown. They are often served with a dipping sauce and enjoyed as a snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish paste (such as cod or haddock)
  • Cornstarch
  • Garlic powder & Onion powder
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions, finely chopped
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  • In a bowl, mix together fish paste, cornstarch, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, soy sauce, sesame oil, and chopped green onions until well combined.
  • Form the mixture into small balls using wet hands to prevent sticking.
  • Heat oil in a deep pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  • Gently drop the fishballs into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-5 minutes.
  • Remove fishballs with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
