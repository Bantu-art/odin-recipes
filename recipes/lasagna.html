lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with a rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. The layers are baked together until the top is golden and bubbly, creating a hearty and comforting casserole. Often enjoyed as a main course, lasagna combines robust flavors and textures, making it a beloved dish worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato paste & Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • Ricotta, Parmesan & Mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Salt &Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a skillet, sauté diced onions and minced garlic in olive oil until translucent.
  • Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  • Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and fresh basil; simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  • Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and a final sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  • Let rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
